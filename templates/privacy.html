Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create a Spendly account, we collect basic information such as your name and email address. As you use the Service, we also store the expense records you enter, including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to provide and improve the Service: authenticating your account, displaying your expenses, and generating spending summaries. We do not sell your personal or financial data to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your account and expense data are stored securely and retained for as long as your account remains active. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us or removing your account, after which your data will be permanently erased.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly may rely on third-party infrastructure providers (such as hosting and email delivery services) to operate the Service. These providers only process data as needed to support Spendly's functionality and are not permitted to use your data for their own purposes.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us through the contact details provided on the Spendly website.
